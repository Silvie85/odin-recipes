Roast Chicken

Description

Classic Roasted Chicken perfect for a Sunday dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Lb. Whole Chicken
  • 2 TBS Olive Oil
  • 1 Bulb whole Garlic
  • 1 yellow Onion
  • 1 Large carrot
  • 3 stalks celery
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 TBS kosher salt
  • 1 TBS Fresh Ground Black Pepper

Method

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 425f
  2. Dry chicken with paper towels
  3. Coat chicken with Olive Oil
  4. Season inside and Outside with Salt and Pepper
  5. Wash and Rough Chop Onions, Carrots, Celery. Split Garlic in half.Stuff vegetables into Cavity of chicken.
  6. Roast for 45 Minutes until Thermometer registers 165f in the thickest part of thigh
